The New Formula 1 Car

The countdown to Formula 1’s new era car launches is underway, as teams put the final touches to their 2022 challengers. But while a showcar created by FOM last year provided us with some insight into the basic form that the new designs will take, teams have been busy developing their own ideas based on what they think can give them the edge over their rivals. That being said, F1’s designers have been more constrained in some areas because the FIA does not want ideas to create closer racing immediately undone.

Red Bull agree deal to run Honda engine technology until 2025

Red Bull and AlphaTauri will continue to race Honda power units from next season through to the end of 2024 after reaching an agreement to run the Japanese technology despite the manufacturer opting to leave the championship at the end of this season. Honda have secured five race victories to date in their three-year partnership with the Red Bull family, but last October announced they would leave F1 when their current agreement expired at the end of 2021, leaving Red Bull and AlphaTauri without an engine supplier.
